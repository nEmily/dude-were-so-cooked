Red Flags
  • Extrapolates from a single anecdote to "10 million jobs" with zero data
  • Writing a resignation letter is not equivalent to performing a job
  • Account posts 40+ "AI doom" tweets per day (engagement farming)

The Real Story LLMs are indeed good at template-based writing tasks. This has no bearing on the claim about 10 million jobs. BLS projects ~152k tech separations for Q1, not 10 million across all sectors.
